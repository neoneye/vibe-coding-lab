2D Tesla Valve

A Tesla valve lets fluid pass easily one way but resists the other — with no moving parts. Forward, particles stream straight down the open lane. Reverse, the diverter tongues scoop them up into the loops, which whirl them around and throw them back against the oncoming flow — the recirculating “backflow” that chokes the reverse direction. Flip the direction and compare the throughput; larger particles get caught more easily.
