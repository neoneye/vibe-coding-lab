# Straight up the Hormuz

Minesweeper × Battleship × oil economy. Clear an enemy fleet out of the Strait
of Hormuz before your oil reserves run dry — every shot costs oil, deduction
numbers point the way, and the Captain's Log narrates your competence level.

Open `index.html`. Click to shoot (1 oil), right-click / long-press to flag.
Recon Flight (3 oil) counts contacts in a 3×3 zone; Bombing Run (5 oil) rakes
a 3-tile line. Sink all 5 ships before the oil gauge hits zero.
